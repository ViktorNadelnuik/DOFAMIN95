About the platoon

We are the technical support platoon of the mechanized infantry battalion of the 142nd Brigade. Our mission is to keep battalion vehicles running on one of the hottest frontline directions.

You rarely see us in the news — but our work decides whether a vehicle reaches positions, whether supplies arrive, and whether evacuation happens on time.

What we do

• Maintenance
Scheduled and urgent maintenance: oils, filters, fluids, inspections — so vehicles are ready anytime.

• Repair & recovery
Engines, transmissions, suspension, brakes, electrics, hydraulics, fuel systems — from sensors to major units.

• Vehicle evacuation
If a vehicle is damaged, stuck or broken — we go for it. Often under fire, pulling it off the “road of life”.

• Diagnostics
We find hidden faults early, so a vehicle doesn’t stop at the worst moment.

• Field repairs
Our workshop is what we can build: ground, tarp, light, generator and tools. No conditions? We create them.

• Keeping the battalion mobile
Working vehicles = rotations, supplies, evacuation, water, food, maneuver and survival.
